About Us

At Sunny Bites Cafe, we believe in the simple joys of good food and great company. Nestled in the heart of the community, our cafe offers a cozy, welcoming space where you can enjoy fresh, flavourful meals made with locally sourced ingredients. From delicious breakfast options to satisfying lunches and mouthwatering desserts. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite, meeting friends, or relaxing with a cup of coffee, Sunny Bites Cafe is your go-to spot for comfort and warmth. Join us for a taste of sunshine in every bite!

Local Chef Makes Waves: Sunny Bites Cafe's Head Chef Wins Regional Culinary Excellence Award

By Sarah Martinez

February 18, 2025 The cozy corner establishment that has been serving our community for the past eight years just added another feather to its cap. Chef Gary Blue, head chef at Sunny Bites Cafe, has been awarded the prestigious Golden Whisk Award for Culinary Innovation in the Southwest Region. Blue, who started his culinary journey in his grandmother's kitchen in New Mexico, beat out 200 other contestants.

From Rock Band to Kitchen Band: Meet Gary Blue, The Chef Who Found His Calling at Sunny Bites

By Alex Chen

If you told Gary Blue ten years ago that he'd become one of the most beloved breakfast chefs in town, he would have laughed while tuning his guitar. The former lead guitarist of local rock band "Midnight Mayhem" now orchestrates a different kind of symphony - one made of sizzling bacon, whisking eggs, and the cheerful chatter of happy customers at Sunny Bites Cafe.
